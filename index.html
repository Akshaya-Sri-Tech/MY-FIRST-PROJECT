TEA

A hot or iced aromatic beverage made by steeping the cured or fresh leaves of the tea plant, Camellia sinensis, in boiling water

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Heat fresh water in a kettle until it reaches a rolling boil.
  2. Pour water over your tea bag or leaves in a mug.
  3. Let it sit undisturbed for 3 to 5 minutes to infuse.
  4. Remove the tea, stir in your sweeteners or milk, and enjoy.
